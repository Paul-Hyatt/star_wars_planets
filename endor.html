Opportunities Abound on Endor!

08/15/3018

To those who are thinking about getting stationed on Endor, if you come from planets known for desert, this might be a welcome change of pace. This place is bright and green and a quiet location in the galaxy. The only thing really of importance is our sheild generator, but the rest of the planet is generally a quiet beautiful place where in the off times you can get out and read under a tree, or lay in a hammock between trees at night. Even better make portions over a campfire with friends. To those interested in ecology and wildlife there seem to be a race or 2 of primitive sentient beings that one of which are called the Ewoks. They seem to fear everyone and are agressive, and are known for their boobytrapping, so stay alert when tracking and viewing this interesting race.
